----------------------------------------------------------------
KNOWN LIMITATIONS
----------------------------------------------------------------

1. CIC-IDS 2017 is simulated — not real-world traffic
2. Only 4 attack types covered (subset of full dataset)
3. Infiltration class severely underrepresented (6 test samples)
4. Model trained on 2017 patterns — modern attacks may differ
5. Real-world threshold requires tuning per deployment environment
6. Destination Port feature may cause brittleness — attackers
   change ports in real scenarios
